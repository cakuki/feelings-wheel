Print on A4 at 100% (“Actual size”). 2 pages: the wheel + a monthly tracker.

How to use it 💛

  1. Find the core feeling in the middle that fits right now.
  2. Move outward to a more exact feeling in the same color.
  3. Say it out loud and talk about when you felt it — no feeling is wrong.
  4. Color one circle a day on the tracker to see the week at a glance.
