How to use it 💛

  1. Print the PDF on A4 at 100% (“Actual size”). Two pages: the wheel and a monthly tracker.
  2. Find the core emotion in the middle that fits, then move outward to a more exact feeling in the same color.
  3. Say it out loud and talk about when you felt it — there is no wrong feeling.
  4. Color one circle a day on the tracker to see the week at a glance.
